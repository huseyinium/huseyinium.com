## Problem

Misyon Bank wanted to onboard retail customers to crypto without overwhelming them. The existing crypto education content was scattered, jargon-heavy, and built for people who already knew what a wallet was. The bank needed a trust-building product that made Bitcoin and Ethereum feel as accessible as a savings account.

## What I Built

A content-driven education platform built with Next.js and Sanity CMS. I designed a progressive disclosure system — newcomers see simple explainers; returning users unlock deeper content as they progress. The content team could publish and update modules without developer involvement. Tailwind CSS v3 with the bank's design system kept the visual language consistent with the main Misyon product.

## Outcome

Launched on schedule. 14,000+ users completed at least one learning module in the first two months. The platform directly supported Misyon's crypto product launch in Q3 2023.
